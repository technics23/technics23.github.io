About Us



TECHNICS

TECHNICS is an annual technical fest event held by Greater Kolkata College of Engineering & Management (GKCEM). The event celebrates innovation, creativity, and technology, and provides a platform for students to showcase their technical skills, participate in competitions and workshops, and learn from experts in the industry. TECHNICS features a range of exciting activities such as coding competitions, robotics challenges, gaming tournaments, and tech quizzes. Participants from different colleges and universities across the region come together to participate in the event, making it a collaborative and competitive experience. One of the main highlights of TECHNICS is the keynote speeches by distinguished professionals in the industry. These speeches provide insights into the latest trends and advancements in technology and inspire students to pursue their interests in the field. Overall, TECHNICS is an excellent opportunity for students to connect with their peers, learn new skills, and have fun while exploring the world of technology.



GKCEM

Greater Kolkata College of Engineering & Management (GKCEM) is a renowned institution in Kolkata, West Bengal, offering high-quality education in engineering, management, and technology. The college has state-of-the-art infrastructure, highly qualified faculty, and a diverse student community. GKCEM organizes TECHNICS, an annual technical fest event, providing a platform for students to showcase their technical skills and learn from experts in the industry. The college also offers scholarships and financial aid programs to deserving students. Overall, GKCEM is an ideal institution for students to pursue their academic and personal goals.
